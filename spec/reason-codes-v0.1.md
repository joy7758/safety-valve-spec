# Reason Codes v0.1

Format: `SVS_<DOMAIN>_<NAME>`

## Receipt / Signature
- SVS_SIG_INVALID_SIGNATURE
- SVS_SIG_MISSING_RECEIPT_SIG
- SVS_SIG_UNSUPPORTED_ALG

## Certificate / Trust
- SVS_CERT_REVOKED
- SVS_CERT_MISSING_CERT
- SVS_CERT_WRONG_ISSUER
- SVS_CERT_INVALID_SIGNATURE
- SVS_CERT_EXPIRED
- SVS_CERT_MISSING_SUBJECT_PK

## Replay / Time
- SVS_REPLAY_DETECTED
- SVS_TIME_EXPIRED_RECEIPT
- SVS_TIME_RECEIPT_TIME_PARSE_ERROR

## Policy / Gate
- SVS_GATE_MISSING_RECEIPT
- SVS_GATE_RECEIPT_PARSE_ERROR
- SVS_GATE_RECEIPT_B64_PARSE_ERROR
- SVS_POLICY_ALLOW
- SVS_POLICY_VIOLATION
- SVS_POLICY_NEEDS_HUMAN_CONFIRM
- SVS_DEGRADE_PREVIEW_ONLY
- SVS_EFFECT_UNKNOWN

## Notes
- DENY/DEGRADE MUST also yield a receipt.
- Implementations MAY add vendor-specific codes, but MUST preserve SVS codes.
